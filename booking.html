MyCharters — Terms & Conditions

All bookings with MyCharters are considered confirmed once the required deposit has been received and the customer receives a booking confirmation. MyCharters reserves the right to refuse or cancel bookings where necessary for operational, safety, or legal reasons.

2. Payment Terms

  • A €100 deposit is required to secure the booking.
  • The remaining balance is paid after the charter unless otherwise agreed in writing.
  • Additional services, fuel surcharges, damages, or overtime may incur extra charges.

3. 24-Hour Cancellation Policy

  • More than 24 hours before departure: full refund of any deposit paid.

6. Customer Responsibilities
